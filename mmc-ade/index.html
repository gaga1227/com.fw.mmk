Mary MacKillop Centre Adelaide is a spiritual oasis in a busy city!

The Centre is located at Kensington, on the historical site of the first Mother House of the Sisters of St Joseph. Established by Mary MacKillop in 1872, this is where she lived, prayed and worked until 1883. Mary MacKillop Centre Adelaide comprises: St Joseph's Chapel, Mary MacKillop Exhibition Centre and Bethany Conference Centre. Together, they form our 'hub of spirituality, education and hospitality'.

Chapel Learn more about the Chapel

Chapel

St Joseph's Chapel: Mary MacKillop's much-loved place of prayer, has become a place of pilgrimage, prayer and peace for people of all backgrounds. Come … breathe in Mary's spirit … pray where she prayed so often … experience an inner peace.

You may wish to write your intentions in a special prayer book so that others can pray for your needs too.

JMHC Hours of Operation

St Joseph's Chapel is open to the public each day from 8.30a.m. – 4.00p.m. Mary MacKillop Exhibition Centre, suitable for both children and adults, is open to individuals and groups from 10am until 4pm, Monday to Thursday. Closed on public holidays, it can be open at other times by negotiation.

Bethany Conference Centre offers adult spirituality programmes throughout the year and is also available for hire by Church–related groups and organisations.

Exhibitions Learn more about our Exhibitions

Exhibitions

Mary MacKillop Exhibition Centre has both permanent and temporary displays. These highlight the early Josephite story in South Australia, and capture the spirit of Mary MacKillop and the pioneer Sisters of St Joseph, who responded to the needs of their times in very practical and human ways.

Visitors are invited to travel back in time as they experience this story, and touch into the spirit of Mary and the first Sisters of St Joseph.

MMC Adelaide Hospitality

Refreshments are available for purchase in the hospitality area of the Exhibition Centre. With advance notice, arrangements can be made to cater for lunches for visiting groups. There is wheelchair access to St Joseph's Chapel, Mary MacKillop Exhibition Centre and Bethany Conference Centre.

Bethany Learn more about Bethany

Bethany

Bethany Conference Centre: Throughout the year, Mary MacKillop Centre Adelaide hosts a whole range of adult spirituality programmes which are held in Bethany. These are open to all.

Our large, versatile space with its modern self–catering and AV facilities, is also available for hire by schools and Church–related groups.

Shop

The shop has a very large range of resources, souvenirs and St Mary MacKillop devotional items. Books suitable for adults as well as children are available.
