Guided Tour Learn more about our Guided Tour

Guided Tour

Experience the life of Mary MacKillop as you journey on a guided tour through the display of dioramas. Life sized models with an informative soundtrack and mood lighting, lead the visitor back in time to experience the courageous spirit and compassionate heart of this woman of God. You will feel the depth of Mary's life commitment.

The visit for students has three distinct activities:

  1. Students are taken on a guided tour through the Mary MacKillop Experience using dioramas with lighting and sound track to create the message of the display.
  2. Students view a display of photographs and memorabilia relating to the story of Mary MacKillop and the Sisters of St Joseph.
  3. A short prayer experience is held in the chapel.

MMC Brisbane Hours of Operation

School groups visits – Monday to Friday. Bookings are essential. Families, small groups, individuals – Please phone or email to arrange a suitable time.
